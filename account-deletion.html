Account Deletion

To delete your TedGift account and all associated data, follow these simple steps:

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the TedGift app
  2. Go to Settings (Profile → Settings)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm deletion
⚠️ Warning: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be immediately deleted.

Data Deleted:

  • Your profile (name, email)
  • All gift recipients you've created
  • All saved gifts and wishlists
  • All gift search history
  • All calendar reminders
  • Purchase history

Data Retained:

None – All data is permanently deleted

Processing Time:

Immediate – Your account is deleted instantly
